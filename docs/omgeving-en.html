Riederalp in every season

From ski slopes to summer hikes with views of the Aletsch Glacier

Discover the Swiss Alps

Rhodania 55 is located in the heart of Riederalp, a car-free mountain village where nature, tranquillity and adventure come together.

Summer Activities

Hiking

Over 300 km of routes through the Alps.

Golf

One of the highest golf courses in Europe.

Mountain Biking

Challenging trails with panoramic views.

Aletsch Glacier

Visit the largest glacier in the Alps, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Swimming in Bettmersee

Refreshing swimming in the beautiful mountain lake on the Bettmeralp.

Children's Playground

Right next to the apartment, perfect for little adventurers.

Winter Activities

Skiing

Direct access to beautiful ski slopes.

Cross-country Skiing

Perfectly maintained trails.

Gondola & Cable Car

Comfortably reach the mountain top with modern gondolas.

Winter Hiking

Enjoy peace and stunning snow landscapes.

Sledging

Classic Swiss winter fun for young and old.

Why visit outside ski season?

  • Peace and relaxation
  • Lower prices
  • Fewer crowds
  • Perfect for hiking and remote work
